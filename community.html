Time with Our Community

Leaders of the Game:

Experience, Leadership, and Legacy

The 1000 Hills Rugby Team is proud of its seasoned players who bring years of experience, leadership, and wisdom to the field. Our adult team members are more than just athletes; they are mentors, role models, and key figures in our community. Their dedication to the game and commitment to fostering a positive environment have made a lasting impact on both the team and our fans. Watch their stories and see how they continue to inspire the next generation of players.

Empowering Girls in Rugby:

Strength, Courage, and Community!

At the 1000 Hills Rugby Team, we celebrate the empowerment of girls through sport. Our girls' program not only focuses on athletic development but also promotes confidence, teamwork, and community involvement. We aim to inspire young women to break barriers and become leaders on and off the field. Join us in supporting our female athletes as they showcase their strength and determination.

Empowering Our Youth:

Building Future Leaders through Rugby!

The 1000 Hills Rugby Team is dedicated to empowering our youth through the sport of rugby. We believe in fostering leadership, teamwork, and resilience in our young athletes. Each training session and match helps build character, instilling values that last a lifetime. Join us in nurturing the next generation of leaders who will carry the spirit of our community forward.
